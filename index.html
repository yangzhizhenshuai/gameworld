Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this action-packed adventure game!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival game where you must fight against endless waves of monsters. Use your skills and strategy to survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups and upgrades.

The game features stunning graphics, intuitive controls, and challenging gameplay that will keep you engaged for hours. How long can you survive?

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby monsters
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose upgrades to enhance your abilities
  • Survive as long as possible!

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from different characters with unique abilities and playstyles.

Upgrade System

Level up and enhance your character with powerful upgrades and abilities.

Endless Gameplay

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters in an endless survival challenge.

High Scores

Compete for the highest survival time and share your achievements.
